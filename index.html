What is Apolaki

Why Choose Apolaki for Solar Installation in the Philippines?

Going solar is a major investment. Choosing the right solar installer is just as important as choosing solar itself. Apolaki gives you everything you need to make the right decision—from finding trusted solar installers and comparing financing options to understanding your potential savings before you commit.

Our promise

Built on trust, not sales pressure

An estimate that's trusted but wrong is worse than no tool at all. So we anchor on your real numbers, show our assumptions, and let installers confirm the figures.

Private until you connect

Your data is encrypted, and only anonymised rooftop metrics reach an installer — never until you choose to connect. Privacy is built into the architecture, compliant with the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173).

Honest, assumption-shown numbers

Every estimate explains what is known, what is assumed, and what an installer must confirm. No hidden averages — your bill and your tariff drive the result.

Measured & verified

Installers re-confirm the numbers against real quotes, and post-installation monitoring checks that promised savings show up. Read our M&V statement →

Our story

A student venture with a simple mission

Apolaki — named for the Filipino god of the sun — was built by three founders at the Asian Institute of Management. Our mission is to make a trusted solar decision as understandable as reviewing a monthly household expense, in service of UN SDG 7: affordable and clean energy.

The word that ties us to SDG 7 is affordable — and we believe affordability begins with clarity.
