For installers

Solar leads in the Philippines that are actually ready to talk

Most leads are not ready to buy — you burn hours before any revenue. Apolaki flips that: homeowners run a bill-backed readiness report first, so by the time they connect with you, they understand their cost, savings and payback. You meet warmer, consented homeowners, not cold names on a list.

Become a partner About Apolaki
Why partner

Warm, consented, and pre-qualified

Apolaki is the neutral readiness layer between homeowners and installers. That means the introductions you get are different from a bought lead list:

  • Consented. The homeowner chose to connect. No scraped numbers, no spam.
  • Pre-qualified. They have already seen a realistic system size and payback for their home.
  • Matched to fit. We introduce homeowners based on your area and specialties, not on who bids highest.
How it works

You focus on quoting and installing

Homeowners do the early education themselves through the assessment. You come in at the point of real intent — to confirm the site, refine the quote and win the install. We are onboarding installer partners region by region; get in touch to join early in your area.

Questions

Installer partner questions

What kind of leads do I get?

Consented homeowners who have run a bill-backed assessment and chosen to connect. They arrive understanding their approximate system size, cost and payback.

How does matching work?

We introduce homeowners based on location, system type and your track record and availability — not on payment. There is no pay-to-rank.

Which areas are you in?

We are onboarding installers region by region, starting with Metro Manila. Reach out and we will tell you honestly where you fit in the rollout.

How do I join?

Use the contact form and choose the installer/partner option, or email us. We will walk you through screening and onboarding.
