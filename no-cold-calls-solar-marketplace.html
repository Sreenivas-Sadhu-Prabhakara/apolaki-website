The problem

Why solar leads become spam

In the usual model, the moment you fill in a form, your number becomes a lead that gets sold to several installers at once. That is why one enquiry turns into weeks of calls and messages. It is bad for you, and it is why so many Filipinos hesitate to look into solar at all.

How Apolaki is different

Your data stays yours

With Apolaki, you get the full picture first — cost, savings, payback, financing — while staying private. We do not sell your details. When you are ready, you choose to connect, and it is a single consented introduction to a screened installer, not your number handed to the highest bidder. If you never want to connect, you never have to.

Questions

Privacy and cold-call questions

Will I get cold calls after using Apolaki?

No. Your details stay private until you choose to connect with an installer, and we never sell your information to lead buyers.

Can I see my solar numbers without giving my number?

Yes. You can run the bill-backed assessment and see your estimated cost, savings and payback while staying anonymous.

What happens when I do connect?

You get a single, consented introduction to a screened installer matched to your home — not your details broadcast to several companies.

How is this different from a Facebook solar group or a lead form?

Those typically expose you to many sellers at once. Apolaki keeps you private, gives you the numbers first, and puts you in control of if and when you connect.
