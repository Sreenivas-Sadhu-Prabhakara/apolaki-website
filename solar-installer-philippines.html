Find a trusted solar installer in the Philippines

The hardest part of going solar is not the panels — it is knowing which installer to trust. Apolaki is the neutral layer between you and the market: run your bill-backed assessment first, then choose to connect with a screened installer matched to your home, on your terms.

How we screen

What “trusted” actually means here

“Verified” should mean something specific, so here is what we check before an installer can be matched with a homeowner:

  • Business registration and a real, contactable presence.
  • Installation portfolio and references from prior work.
  • Licensing and permit track record for grid-tied systems and net metering.
  • No pay-to-rank. Recommendations are based on fit to your home, not on who paid the most.

Our installer network is growing region by region. Where we do not yet have a screened partner in your area, we tell you honestly rather than matching you with someone unvetted.

On your terms

You stay in control of your data

You see your full readiness report before any installer sees you. Your contact details stay private until you choose to connect, and when you do, it is a warm, consented introduction — never a lead sold to whoever bids highest. That is the difference between a marketplace and a lead broker.

Questions

Installer matching questions

How do you choose which installer to recommend?

By fit to your home and bill, not by who paid. We factor in system type, your location and each installer's track record and availability. There is no pay-to-rank.

Will installers cold-call me?

No. Your details stay private until you choose to connect. Apolaki exists partly to end the spam-call problem, not add to it.

Do you have installers everywhere in the Philippines?

Not yet. Coverage is expanding city by city, starting with Metro Manila. Where we do not have a screened installer near you, we say so honestly.

Is there a cost to get matched?

No. Matching and your assessment are free for homeowners. Installers partner with us to reach ready, consented homeowners.
